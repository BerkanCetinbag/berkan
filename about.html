BERKAN CETINBAG

I'm Berkan, a 24-year-old architect and freelance artist with a lifelong passion for drawing, focusing on fantasy art and character design. Originally from Turkey, I grew up in the UAE, and now live in Spain, where I continue to refine and evolve my art and architecture.

My background as an architect has made me a top-tier designer, giving me a strong foundation in structure, precision, and detail, qualities that shine through in my art. This blend of skills allows me to create intricate, immersive pieces with a level of detail and realism that sets my work apart.

When I work on commissions, I make it a priority to collaborate closely with my clients. I provide daily updates and encourage ongoing feedback to ensure the final result is exactly what the client envisioned. Feel free to contact me at berkancetinbag@gmail.com to learn more about my art or discuss a potential project.
